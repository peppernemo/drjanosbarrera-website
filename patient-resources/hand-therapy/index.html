Hand Therapy

Finding a Certified Hand Therapist (CHT)

Specialized rehabilitation for the hand and upper extremity. Working with a CHT often means faster recovery and better outcomes.

What is a CHT?

A CHT is an occupational therapist (OT) or physical therapist (PT) with advanced training in the hand and upper extremity: 3+ years of clinical experience, 4,000+ hours of hand therapy, a national certification exam, and recertification every 5 years.

National CHT Directory

htcc.org/find-a-cht — search for certified hand therapists anywhere in the country.
